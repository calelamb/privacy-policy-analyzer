PRIVACY POLICY FOR EDULEARN APP

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. DATA COLLECTION
We collect the following types of information:
- Personal Information: Student name, grade level, school name, parent/guardian email
- Usage Data: Learning progress, quiz scores, time spent on activities
- Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers

2. DATA USE PURPOSE
We use collected data ONLY for:
- Providing educational services and personalized learning experiences
- Communicating with parents about student progress
- Improving our educational content
We DO NOT use student data for targeted advertising or behavioral marketing.

3. THIRD-PARTY SHARING
We share data with the following third parties:
- AWS (Amazon Web Services): For secure cloud storage
- SendGrid: For parent email communications
- Google Analytics: For aggregated usage statistics (no PII shared)
We do not sell or rent student data to any third parties.

4. PARENTAL CONSENT
For users under 13, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information. Parents can provide consent through:
- Digital consent form with email verification
- School acting as agent for parents (where permitted)

5. LEGAL COMPLIANCE
We comply with:
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)
- State student privacy laws

6. DATA RETENTION
We retain student data only as long as necessary:
- Active accounts: Data retained during use
- Inactive accounts: Deleted after 18 months of inactivity
- Upon request: Data deleted within 30 days

7. USER RIGHTS
Parents and eligible students have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Request corrections to inaccurate data
- Delete their account and associated data
- Opt-out of certain data uses
Contact privacy@edulearn.com to exercise these rights.

8. DATA SECURITY
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption of data at rest (AES-256)
- Regular security audits
- Limited employee access with authentication
- Secure data centers with physical controls

9. TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES
We use limited tracking technologies:
- Session cookies for login functionality
- Analytics cookies to understand usage patterns
- No third-party advertising cookies
Users can disable cookies in browser settings, though some features may not function properly.
